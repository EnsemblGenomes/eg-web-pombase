About the Schizosaccharomyces japonicus genome

Schizosaccharomyces japonicus, unlike the other Schizosaccaromycae species, rapidly adopts a hyphal growth form

Assembly

The genomic assembly used comes from the Schizosaccharomyces Comparative Genome Project, Broad Institute of Harvard and MIT, described here, and deposited in INSDC as Genome Assembly GCA_000149845.2 (WGS project AATM2000000).
